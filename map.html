Mi Isla del Encanto: let me show you my island.

Not many people are aware that Puerto Rico is an archipelago, it is also home to the only federally protected rain forest in the United States you don't need a passport to visit, it is a commonwealth of the US, so we use the American Dollar. If you love the christmas time of year, I'd highly suggest visiting in the month of December. Flights will be a little more expensive but the atmosphere, the food, the music, the people. you'll fall in love. come check out the island life!
